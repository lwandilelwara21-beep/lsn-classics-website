About LSN Classics

A safe home away from home for students.

LSN Classics Pty Ltd is a premium student accommodation provider in South Africa, created for students who value safety, comfort, and a polished living environment. We offer professionally managed residences where academic success, personal growth, and peace of mind are at the centre of everyday life.

Our spaces are designed to feel elegant yet welcoming, with thoughtful amenities, secure access, and a strong sense of community. Whether you are a first-year student or a senior scholar, you will find a home that supports your goals and gives your family confidence.

FAQ

Questions parents and students often ask.

Is WiFi included?

Yes. High-speed WiFi is included in the monthly rent to support studying, research and staying connected.

Is parking available?

Yes. Secure parking is available for residents and approved visitors.

Are visitors allowed?

Visitors are welcome during designated hours, and all guest visits are managed for resident safety.

How do I apply?

Complete the application form below and our team will guide you through availability, pricing and next steps.

Our residence is conveniently positioned close to several universities and major transport routes.
